About this library

Sasani Music is a free, open library of sheet music arrangements paired with MP3 audio examples, built to make music more accessible to everyone. Spanning many genres and eras, the arrangements here cover a wide variety of instruments — from tin whistles and recorders to kalimba and xiao.

All arrangements are free to use, share, and adapt for non-commercial purposes under the Creative Commons license below.
